Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

AdBox ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and a username derived from it.
  • Device Information: We collect device model, operating system version, and device identifiers for anti-fraud purposes.
  • Usage Data: We track app interactions including game sessions, focus sessions, ad views, missions completed, and earning activity.
  • Advertising ID: Collected by our ad partners (AdMob, Start.io) for serving personalized ads.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To calculate earnings, streaks, levels, and rewards
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To serve relevant advertisements
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or support requests

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication, database, and storage. Your account data is stored on Supabase servers.
  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising ID.
  • Start.io: Used for ad mediation. Start.io may collect device information and location data.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and crash reporting.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (Supabase, Google Cloud)
  • Advertising partners (AdMob, Start.io) as necessary to serve ads
  • Law enforcement if required by applicable law

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Ad partners retain data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your data within the app (profile, earnings, history).
  • Correction: You can update your GCash details in the app settings.
  • Deletion: Contact us to request account deletion.
  • Opt-out: You may disable crash analytics and sound effects in Settings.

Children's Privacy

AdBox is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
